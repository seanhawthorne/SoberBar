Questions

Reservation FAQs.

Do I need a reservation?

Not required — walk-ins are always welcome. But reservations are recommended for Friday and Saturday evenings, and for groups of 4+.

How many people can I reserve for?

Up to 12 guests online. For larger groups or private events, email Sean@sipsoberbar.com and we'll make it work.

Can I choose indoor or outdoor?

Yes. When SeatOn is live, you'll select indoor or outdoor at booking. We'll always try to accommodate your preference.

What's your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 2 hours before your reservation. Late cancellations and no-shows may affect future bookings.

Do you host private events?

We do. The full space accommodates up to 50 guests. Email Sean@sipsoberbar.com with your date and group size.
