Florida Guide

Best Non-Alcoholic & Mocktail Bars in Florida

The sober-curious movement is real, and Florida is finally getting spaces that take alcohol-free drinking seriously. Here's where to find real craft, real atmosphere, and zero alcohol.

This isn't a list of places that will hand you a soda and call it a mocktail. These are the Florida spots — dedicated sober bars, spirit-free speakeasies, and serious restaurant bars — building drinks with the same glassware, garnish, and intention as any cocktail bar.

Know a spot we missed? Email us and we'll keep the guide updated.

Dedicated Alcohol-Free Bars

GulfportZero-Proof CocktailsCoffee House

Sober Bar

3062 Beach Blvd S, Gulfport, FL 33707 · sipsoberbar.com · @sipsoberbar

Sober Bar is Gulfport's zero-proof cocktail bar and coffee house. Mornings bring specialty espresso, house-made Kyoto cold brew, and a New Orleans-inspired kitchen. After dark the room shifts to a candlelit speakeasy serving craft non-alcoholic cocktails, NA beer, and kombucha. Nothing on the menu contains alcohol. Try the Call Me an Ube or the Jinn & Juniper.

OrlandoSpirit-Free Speakeasy

The BANDBOX

Orlando, FL · bandboxorlando.com · @bandboxorlando

Central Florida's first completely spirit-free speakeasy. The BANDBOX built its reputation on craft non-alcoholic cocktails in a hidden, reservations-only setting. Originally in Ivanhoe Village, the concept is set to reopen in Orlando's Mills 50 district.

JacksonvilleAlcohol-Free Lounge

57 Heaven

8136 Atlantic Blvd, Jacksonville, FL 32211 · facebook.com/57Heaven

An alcohol-free lounge and social spot in Jacksonville offering a night-out atmosphere without the booze. Check their schedule for Latin nights and live events.

JacksonvilleBooze-Free Bar

Wildcrafters

Jacksonville, FL · @wildcraftersjax

Jacksonville's first booze-free bar, known for kava, teas, and craft non-alcoholic cocktails. The original Park Street location closed in 2025; follow them for relocation news.

MiamiKava + Mocktails

Syndicate Kava Bar

2451 NW 5th Ave, Miami, FL 33127 · syndicatekavabar.com · @syndicatewynwood

Wynwood's plant-based, non-alcoholic bar serving kava, kratom, and creative mocktails. A go-to for Miami's sober-curious and wellness crowd.

Best Mocktail Menus at Regular Bars

These spots still serve alcohol, but their mocktail programs are worth the trip for anyone not drinking.

St. PetersburgMocktails

Cassis, Copa, The Dog Bar, Flute & Dram, Sesh Brewing, Golden Isles Brewing, The Ship's Hold

St. Pete's dining scene takes mocktails seriously. Cassis has a dedicated mocktail section; Copa and The Dog Bar each carry multiple NA options; Flute & Dram overlooks the water with an NA mojito; Sesh Brewing and Golden Isles Brewing offer craft NA beer; The Ship's Hold pairs a laid-back atmosphere with solid alcohol-free drinks.

Source: I Love the Burg — St. Pete's best bars for mocktails and non-alcoholic drinks

OrlandoDate Night

Santiago's & West End Trading Co.

Orlando's alcohol-free date-night scene includes inventive mocktails at Santiago's and a range of spirit-free options at West End Trading Co. See the full round-up at Orlando Date Night Guide.

Why This List Exists

Sober Bar was built because the ritual of going out matters — whether you're sober, sober-curious, the designated driver, pregnant, in recovery, or just taking a night off. We keep this guide updated because the more great alcohol-free options Florida has, the easier it is for everyone to choose clarity without staying home.

Planning a trip through Florida? Start in Gulfport. Sober Bar is open daily at 7am, five minutes from St. Pete Beach, with the full kitchen, espresso bar, and zero-proof cocktail menu running open to close.
