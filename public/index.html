The Rhythm

Morning coffee,
evening energy.

Mornings start quietly — cortados, lattes, and breakfast sandwiches while the sun hits Beach Boulevard. The soundtrack is low, the light is soft, and the WiFi is fast.

As the day shifts, so does the room. The lighting warms up, the music finds its groove, and the bar comes alive with craft zero-proof cocktails built with the same precision as any top-shelf bar.

Designed to work for a 7 AM coffee meeting and a Friday night that runs till 1 AM. Same room, different energy.

Sober Bar is Gulfport's zero-proof cocktail bar, coffee spot, and gathering space. We serve craft zero-proof cocktails, New Orleans inspired food, NA beer & wine, and specialty coffee — all in a moody, design-forward setting on Beach Boulevard.

We're open daily: Sunday through Thursday 7 AM – 10 PM, and Friday & Saturday 7 AM – 1 AM. Coffee and breakfast start at 7 AM every day. The full food and drink menu is available from open to close.

Yes. Every drink on our menu is 100% zero-proof. Our cocktails are built with NA spirits, fresh ingredients, and real craft. Our beer and wine selections are all non-alcoholic. Full experience, zero alcohol.
