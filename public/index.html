The Cocktails

Full ritual,
zero proof.

Every drink is built like it belongs on a top-shelf menu — layered, garnished, and worth slowing down for. NA spirits, fresh ingredients, real craft. Nothing on our menu contains alcohol.

Each signature pour is the work of one of our own bartenders, built on house syrups and fresh-pressed juice. Ask about adding functional botanicals to any beverage.

Questions

Before you come through.

Is everything really alcohol-free?

Yes — 100%. Cocktails are built on NA spirits with fresh ingredients, and every beer and kombucha we pour is non-alcoholic. Full experience, zero proof.

What are your hours?

Open daily. Sunday through Thursday 7 am – 10 pm, Friday and Saturday 7 am – midnight. Coffee and breakfast start at 7 am; the full menu runs open to close.

Can I work here during the day?

Absolutely. Mornings and afternoons are coffee-house energy — fast WiFi, good seats, great espresso. The room shifts to bar energy as the sun goes down.

Do you serve food all day?

Yes. Breakfast sandwiches, bagels, flatbreads, muffulettas, and shareable plates from open to close — pretzel bites at 11 pm on a Saturday are encouraged.
