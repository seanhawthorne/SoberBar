FAQ

How do you keep businesses from buying the Home Edition PDF?
We gate it with: (1) mandatory “non‑commercial use” agreement, (2) purchase with personal email only, (3) per‑buyer watermarking, and (4) expiring download links. The PDF only lists consumer‑friendly sources (no EIN / no MOQ), so the pricing is naturally less attractive for a bar.
What does “vetted” mean for suppliers?
Business credentials, COAs, delivery history, and reputation checks. We monitor ongoing performance and remove low performers.
Do you list kratom sources?
Yes, where legally permitted. Members are responsible for complying with local/state laws.
