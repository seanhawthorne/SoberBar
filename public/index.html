FAQ

How do you keep businesses from buying the Home Edition PDF?
We gate it with: (1) mandatory “non‑commercial use” agreement, (2) purchase with personal email only, (3) per‑buyer watermarking, and (4) expiring download links. The PDF only lists consumer‑friendly sources (no EIN / no MOQ), so the pricing is naturally less attractive for a bar.
What does “vetted” mean for suppliers?
We validate licensing and documents, verify COAs for relevant SKUs, review delivery performance and dispute history, assess reputation, and monitor ongoing performance.
How often are prices updated?
Tracked SKUs are reviewed routinely. We surface price moves and note MOQ/EIN changes so you can act with current info.
Can you intro us to the right supplier?
Yes—Professional includes one warm introduction per month to a vetted supplier that matches your SKU/volume needs.
Will you review a quote we received?
Yes—Professional includes one monthly SKU quote review. We sanity‑check against current market and flag negotiation levers.
Do you list kratom sources?
Yes, where legally permitted. Members are responsible for complying with local/state laws.
Do you have affiliate relationships?
For a few suppliers we may use affiliate links. These never change the price you pay and don’t affect how we rank or present suppliers.
