Questions

Before you come through.

Is everything really alcohol-free?

Yes — 100%. Cocktails are built on NA spirits with fresh ingredients, and every beer and wine we pour is non-alcoholic. Full experience, zero proof.

What are your hours?

Open daily. Sunday through Thursday 7 am – 10 pm, Friday and Saturday 7 am – 1 am. Coffee and breakfast start at 7 am; the full menu runs open to close.

Can I work here during the day?

Absolutely. Mornings and afternoons are coffee-house energy — fast WiFi, good seats, great espresso. The room shifts to bar energy as the sun goes down.

Do you serve food all day?

Yes. Breakfast sandwiches, bagels, flatbreads, muffalettas, and shareable plates from open to close — pretzel bites at 11 pm are encouraged.
