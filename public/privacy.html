Privacy Policy.

What our mailing-list form collects, why, and how we handle it.

Last updated: July 2026

Information We Collect

The only information we collect through this site is an email address, and only if you choose to enter it in our mailing-list form. We don't ask for anything else.

How the Form Works

When you submit the form, your browser opens a pre-filled email in your own mail app addressed to us — you send it yourself. The website itself does not store your email or transmit it to any server or third-party service.

How We Use Your Information

If you email us to join the list, we use your address only to send occasional updates about Sober Bar. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with anyone. Reply to any message to be removed, or email Sean@sipsoberbar.com anytime.

Cookies & Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics on this site.
