Privacy Policy

A simple overview of what we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle information shared through the Sober Bar site.

Last updated: May 2025

Information We Collect

When you sign up for our opening list or soft opening invitations, we collect your name, email address, and optionally your Instagram handle. We collect this only when you voluntarily submit it.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to send you updates about Sober Bar's opening, invitations to events, and announcements. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Storage

Form submissions are stored locally and used only for communication related to Sober Bar. If you wish to have your information removed, email Sean@sipsoberbar.com.

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Any form data is stored in your browser's local storage.

Contact

Questions? Email Sean@sipsoberbar.com.
