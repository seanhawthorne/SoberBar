Why alcohol-free events work

  • Inclusive. Everyone can participate — DDs, pregnant guests, people in recovery, religious abstainers, or anyone taking a night off.
  • Memorable. Guests remember the drinks and the atmosphere, not the hangover.
  • Productive. Corporate groups stay sharp and conversational.
  • Lower risk. No liquor liability, no overservice, no drama.

What guests drink

Our bar program uses non-alcoholic spirits, wines, beers, fresh juices, bitters, shrubs, and house syrups. Popular builds include the Call Me an Ube, Jinn & Juniper, and Satsuma-Basil Smash. We can design a custom cocktail for your event or brand.
