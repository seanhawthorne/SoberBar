Best Mocktails in St. Petersburg and Gulfport

July 2026 • Sober Bar, Gulfport, FL

The mocktail scene in Tampa Bay is exploding. What used to mean a Shirley Temple at a wedding now means smoked glassware, house-made bitters, and bartenders who take your drink as seriously as any cocktail.

If you're looking for the best mocktails in St. Petersburg and Gulfport, here's where to start.

1. Sober Bar — Gulfport

Gulfport's first fully alcohol-free cocktail bar. The menu is built like a real cocktail list because it is one. Favorites include:

  • Smoked Old Fashioned — bitters, smoke, citrus peel, zero alcohol.
  • Espresso Martini — bold, foamy, no crash.
  • Melo Mule — ginger-forward and refreshing.
  • Kava Cloud — for something botanical and calming.

Located at 3062 Beach Blvd S, Sober Bar is a short walk from the Gulfport Pier and a natural stop before or after Art Walk.

2. Local Coffee Shops with Mocktails

Several St. Pete coffee shops now offer elevated non-alcoholic options, especially during evening hours. Look for spots serving shrubs, tonic-based drinks, and alcohol-free aperitifs.

3. Restaurants with Zero-Proof Lists

A growing number of Tampa Bay restaurants now stock non-alcoholic spirits like Seedlip, Ritual, and Lyre's. Ask your server for the NA cocktail list — many don't advertise it unless you ask.

What Makes a Great Mocktail?

A great mocktail isn't just soda and juice. It's:

  • Balanced — sweet, sour, bitter, and aromatic.
  • Complex — house syrups, bitters, shrubs, smoke, or infusions.
  • Presented well — proper glassware, garnish, ice.
  • Satisfying — it should feel like a ritual, not a compromise.

Why Gulfport Is Becoming the Mocktail Capital of Tampa Bay

Gulfport has always been a little different — artsy, independent, and community-first. An alcohol-free cocktail bar fits the neighborhood perfectly: inclusive, creative, and unafraid to try something new.
