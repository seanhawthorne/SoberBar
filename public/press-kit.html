About

Sober Bar is Gulfport's first fully alcohol-free cocktail bar and coffee house. We serve zero-proof cocktails, kava drinks, kratom seltzer, espresso, and craft non-alcoholic beverages in a bar atmosphere that feels like a real night out — because it is.

We built Sober Bar for people who don't drink, are sober-curious, or just want a great drink that won't slow them down tomorrow. No alcohol. No hangovers. No judgment.
