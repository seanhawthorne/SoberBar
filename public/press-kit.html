Press Kit

Sober Bar is Gulfport's zero-proof cocktail bar and coffee house — a coffee house by day, a speakeasy by night, all alcohol-free.

The Story

Sober Bar opened at 3062 Beach Blvd S in Gulfport, Florida to prove that going out doesn't require alcohol. The room is art-deco and candlelit. The drinks are built like craft cocktails. The kitchen draws from New Orleans. The espresso program rivals any specialty café. The result is a place where designated drivers, the sober-curious, people in recovery, pregnant guests, remote workers, and anyone who wants the ritual without the proof can have a full night out.
